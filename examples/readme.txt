***************************
Description of the examples
***************************

*** Example 1 ***

Example 1 is a complete example of an application that 
collects and visualises traces.

This example gives a good overview of what Samotraces can 
offer. In order to learn how to use Samotraces, please refer
to TODO.

*** Example 2 ***

Example 2 is a more simple example of an application.
This example works out of the box and does not require to
have a ktbs installed (a random trace is generated).

*** Example 3 ***

Example 3 visualises traces of music. It shows how the 
visualisation widget can be used to display music notes.
